What Can You Donate?

Note: Each e-waste type earns different points when donated. Click a category to see how many points you can earn!

Monitors & Displays

Screens, TVs, tablets

CPU & Motherboards

Computers, motherboards

Mobile Devices

Phones, tablets, watches

Audio Equipment

Speakers, headphones

Cables & Accessories

Cables, chargers, adapters

Before Donating

  • Remove all personal data and perform factory reset
  • Remove batteries when possible
  • Keep original accessories together

What We Accept

  • Working and non-working electronics
  • Computers, phones, tablets, accessories
  • Small appliances and gaming devices

Not Accepted

  • Large appliances (refrigerators, washers)
  • Hazardous materials or chemicals
  • Items with personal data not wiped
